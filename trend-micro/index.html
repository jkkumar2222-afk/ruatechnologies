Trend Micro Suite

Trend Micro endpoint, email, workload, and XDR security from one vendor.

Rua Technologies delivers enterprise IT solutions including Microsoft 365, Azure, cybersecurity, cloud migration, managed IT services, and digital transformation tailored to modern businesses.

Rua Technologies is an authorized Trend Micro partner, helping businesses across Bhopal, Indore, Nagpur, the NCR, and the rest of India license and deploy the right tier of Trend Micro protection, connect it to their existing Microsoft 365 or Google Workspace environment, and keep it under one accountable team.

Most Trend Micro engagements start with one of three needs: replacing an aging or unmanaged antivirus setup with a cloud-managed console, adding hosted email security in front of Exchange Online or Gmail to catch phishing and business email compromise, or protecting cloud and on-prem servers with workload security that scales across physical, virtual, and cloud instances. We scope the right products, handle deployment, and tune policies so protection doesn't get in the way of day-to-day work.

Plans

Business and enterprise security tiers for every environment.

We help you choose the right Trend Micro products, deploy the agents and consoles, and tune policies for your environment.

Business plans

Worry-Free Services

Business · SMB endpoint security

Cloud-managed antivirus and endpoint protection built for small businesses without a dedicated IT security team. It's the right starting point for organizations that need real protection without running their own on-site management server.

IncludedAntivirus, ransomware protection, web threat protection, cloud-based console
  • Best for small businesses
  • No on-site server required
  • Simple cloud console
  • Fast deployment across existing devices
Popular

Worry-Free Services Advanced

Business · Endpoint + email security

Everything in Worry-Free Services plus hosted email security for Exchange Online and hosted mailboxes. Most small businesses land here once they want endpoint and email protection managed from a single console.

IncludedEndpoint protection, Exchange/hosted email security, spam and phishing filtering
  • Best for small businesses
  • Adds email security layer
  • Single console for endpoints and mail
  • Works alongside Microsoft 365 or hosted email

Worry-Free Business Security

Business · On-premises endpoint security

The on-premises counterpart to Worry-Free Services, with a security server hosted on your own network instead of the cloud. This suits small businesses with limited or unreliable internet connectivity, or a preference for keeping the management server in-house.

IncludedOn-premises security server, antivirus, ransomware protection, web threat protection
  • Best for offline or low-connectivity sites
  • Management server hosted on-premises
  • Same core protection as the cloud version
  • Good fit where data residency rules require on-site control

Enterprise plans

Apex One

Enterprise · Endpoint protection & EDR

Enterprise endpoint protection with detection and response capabilities for larger, more complex environments. This is where organizations that have outgrown basic antivirus and need investigation and response tooling land.

IncludedEDR, ransomware protection, vulnerability protection, endpoint sensor
  • No user minimum by request
  • On-prem or SaaS deployment
  • Integrates with Vision One XDR
  • Vulnerability protection for unpatched systems

Cloud One – Workload Security

Enterprise · Server & cloud workload protection

Server and cloud workload protection covering physical, virtual, and cloud instances from a single platform. It suits organizations running mixed environments — some servers on-prem, some in AWS or Azure — who want one policy set across all of them.

IncludedAnti-malware, intrusion prevention, integrity monitoring, application control
  • Covers physical, virtual, and cloud servers
  • Pay-as-you-go or subscription licensing
  • Supports AWS, Azure, and on-prem
  • Intrusion prevention without a hardware appliance

Email Security

Enterprise · Hosted email protection

Cloud-based email security that filters spam, phishing, and business email compromise before it reaches Exchange or Gmail. It sits in front of your existing mailbox provider, so no MX record ownership changes are needed at the mailbox level.

IncludedAnti-spam, anti-phishing, BEC protection, sandbox malware analysis
  • Works with Microsoft 365 and Google Workspace
  • Business email compromise (BEC) detection
  • No on-site hardware required
  • Sandbox analysis for suspicious attachments

Vision One

Enterprise · XDR & unified security

A unified detection and response platform correlating signals across endpoints, email, servers, and cloud workloads. This is the layer that ties your other Trend Micro products together into one investigation timeline instead of separate consoles.

IncludedCross-layer detection and response (XDR), threat investigation, automated response
  • Correlates alerts across the environment
  • Reduces investigation time
  • Add-on to existing Trend Micro products
  • Automated response playbooks

Apex Central

Enterprise · Centralized security management

A single management console for Apex One and related endpoint products, giving IT teams one place to see policy status, alerts, and compliance across the whole endpoint fleet. It's the layer that keeps multi-site or multi-team deployments from turning into separate, disconnected consoles.

IncludedCentralized policy management, compliance reporting, threat intelligence sharing
  • Single console across endpoint deployments
  • Compliance and audit reporting
  • Reduces admin overhead on multi-site rollouts
  • Pairs directly with Apex One

Deep Discovery

Enterprise · Network threat detection

Network-level threat detection that inspects traffic for advanced malware and lateral movement that endpoint tools alone can miss. It's typically added where compliance or risk requirements call for network-layer visibility on top of endpoint and email protection.

IncludedNetwork traffic inspection, sandbox analysis, lateral movement detection
  • Detects threats endpoint tools alone can miss
  • Sandbox analysis for suspicious network traffic
  • Feeds detections into Vision One
  • Suited to compliance-driven environments

Deployment

How we deploy and manage Trend Micro for your environment.

Endpoint rollout

Agent deployment

Agent deployment across existing Windows, macOS, and Linux devices, with policy groups matched to how different teams actually work. We stage rollouts so protection lands without disrupting active users mid-day.

CoversAgent deployment, policy group design, exclusion tuning
  • Staged rollout across device fleets
  • Policy groups by department or risk level
  • Exclusion tuning to reduce false positives
  • Console access handed to your admins

Email security setup

Mail flow & filtering

Mail flow configuration in front of Exchange Online or Gmail, with spam, phishing, and BEC filtering tuned to your organization's traffic. We adjust sensitivity thresholds over the first weeks to cut false positives without letting threats through.

CoversMail flow/MX configuration, policy tuning, quarantine review setup
  • Works alongside Microsoft 365 or Google Workspace
  • Quarantine and release workflow configured
  • BEC and phishing policy tuning
  • Sandbox analysis for attachments enabled

Workload & server protection

Cloud & on-prem servers

Deployment across physical servers, VMs, and cloud instances with a single policy set that follows workloads wherever they run. We configure intrusion prevention and integrity monitoring so change detection doesn't flood admins with noise.

CoversAgent deployment on servers, policy design, integrity monitoring baselines
  • Covers AWS, Azure, and on-prem servers
  • Intrusion prevention rules tuned per workload
  • Integrity monitoring baseline configuration
  • Application control policy design

XDR & investigation

Vision One setup

Connecting your Trend Micro products into Vision One so endpoint, email, and server alerts correlate into a single investigation timeline. We configure automated response playbooks for common threat patterns to cut manual triage time.

CoversVision One onboarding, data source connection, playbook configuration
  • Cross-product alert correlation
  • Automated response playbook setup
  • Reduces manual investigation time
  • Works across endpoint, email, and workload products
