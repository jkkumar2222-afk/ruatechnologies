Plans

Business and enterprise plans for every team size.

We help you choose the right Google Workspace edition, migrate mailboxes safely, and configure the admin console for security and compliance.

Business plans

Business Starter

Business · Entry-level

Custom business email plus core collaboration tools for small teams that need the basics done well. It's the right starting point if you mainly need reliable email, video calls, and shared documents without advanced security add-ons.

IncludedGmail Business (30GB pooled storage), Google Meet (100 participants), Docs, Sheets, Slides, Chat
  • Best for up to 300 users
  • Standard support included
  • Lowest-cost Workspace plan
  • Easy upgrade path to Standard or Plus as needs grow
  • 30GB pooled storage per user
  • Basic admin console and 2-Step Verification
  • No Vault, DLP, or advanced endpoint management
  • Google Workspace Migrate tool for onboarding
  • Gemini AI assistant included across Gmail, Docs, and Meet
  • 14-day free trial available

Business Standard

Business · More storage & meetings

Everything in Business Starter with larger mail storage, longer meeting recordings, and booking tools. This is the plan most growing teams settle on once mailbox storage and meeting limits start getting in the way.

IncludedGmail Business (2TB pooled storage), Google Meet recording, Appointment booking, standard support
  • Best for up to 300 users
  • Meeting recordings saved to Google Drive
  • Shared drives for teams
  • Appointment scheduling built into Calendar
  • 2TB pooled storage per user
  • Up to 150 meeting participants
  • Common migration point from Microsoft 365 Business Standard
  • Vault and advanced endpoint management not included
  • Gemini AI access across every app, not just Gmail
  • HIPAA Business Associate Agreement available

Business Plus

Business · Security & compliance

Adds enhanced security, eDiscovery, and retention controls on top of Business Standard for growing teams. It's the entry point for organizations that need to hold onto records for legal or regulatory reasons, or manage devices connecting to company data.

IncludedGmail Business (5TB pooled storage), Google Vault for eDiscovery, enhanced endpoint management
  • Best for up to 300 users
  • Data retention and eDiscovery
  • Advanced endpoint management
  • Custom email routing and rules
  • 5TB pooled storage per user
  • Enhanced security with Vault and endpoint management
  • Context-Aware Access for conditional app access
  • Up to 150 meeting participants with recording
  • HIPAA BAA plus Vault covers most compliance needs
  • Advanced Gemini features across Docs, Sheets, and Meet

Enterprise plans

Enterprise Starter

Enterprise · Communication only

A lower-cost enterprise entry point focused on secure business email and messaging, without the full suite. It suits deskless or frontline staff who need a company email address and Chat access but not document editing tools.

IncludedGmail Business, Chat, Google Meet — no Docs/Sheets/Slides included by default
  • No user minimum by request
  • Enterprise-grade security controls
  • Good fit for frontline communication
  • Can be mixed with higher tiers for office staff
  • Gmail Business and Chat included
  • No Docs/Sheets/Slides by default
  • Enterprise-tier admin console and support SLAs
  • Custom pricing based on user count and usage
  • No 300-user combined cap that applies to Business plans
  • Custom-negotiated pricing, not self-serve checkout

Enterprise Standard

Enterprise · Full collaboration suite

The core enterprise plan with the full collaboration suite, larger meetings, and advanced admin controls. Most mid-size and larger organizations land here once they need custom user counts and finer-grained admin policy control than the Business tiers allow.

IncludedGmail Business (5TB pooled storage), Google Meet (150 participants + recording), Google Vault, advanced admin controls
  • Custom user counts
  • Enhanced security and compliance
  • Priority support available
  • Granular admin console policy groups
  • 5TB pooled storage per user
  • Up to 150 meeting participants with recording
  • Google Vault included for eDiscovery
  • S/MIME encryption not included — available on Enterprise Plus
  • No user minimum or maximum, unlike Business tiers
  • Dedicated support tier above Business plans

Enterprise Plus

Enterprise · Advanced security & analytics

The top-tier plan, adding DLP, advanced endpoint management, S/MIME encryption, and Work Insights. This is where organizations with strict data-protection or regulatory obligations land, since DLP and S/MIME give you the controls auditors typically ask for.

IncludedGmail Business (5TB pooled storage), Google Meet (500 participants + noise cancellation), DLP, S/MIME, BigQuery export
  • Custom user counts
  • Data loss prevention (DLP)
  • 24/7 premium support
  • Security Center for org-wide threat visibility
  • Up to 500 meeting participants with noise cancellation
  • S/MIME encryption and BigQuery export
  • Work Insights for adoption reporting
  • Priority 24/7 support with defined SLAs
  • Meetings scale up to 1,000 participants with live streaming to 10,000 viewers
  • Dedicated customer success manager

Hosted email

Custom-domain business email with spam filtering, mobile sync, and pooled or per-user mailbox storage. We handle MX record setup, SPF/DKIM/DMARC configuration to keep your mail out of spam folders, and staged cutover so inboxes don't go dark mid-migration.

CoversDomain and DNS setup, alias and group configuration, migration from legacy email
  • Custom domain email
  • Migration from Microsoft 365 or on-prem mail
  • Storage varies by plan
  • SPF, DKIM, and DMARC configured for deliverability

Google Chat

Team messaging

Persistent group messaging and spaces that sit alongside Meet and Gmail instead of a bolted-on third-party app. We set up spaces by team or project, configure guest access for external partners, and connect the apps and bots your team already relies on.

CoversSpace structure, guest access setup, bot and app integrations
  • Spaces for teams and projects
  • External guest access controls
  • Google Workspace app integrations

Google Calendar

Scheduling & booking

Shared calendars, resource booking, and appointment scheduling built into every plan. We configure resource calendars for meeting rooms and equipment, set sharing defaults so free/busy information is visible without exposing details, and enable booking pages for client-facing teams.

CoversResource calendar setup, sharing permission defaults, appointment booking pages
  • Shared and resource calendars
  • Meeting room and equipment booking
  • Free/busy visibility controls

Google Meet

Video meetings

Video conferencing with recording, noise cancellation, and participant limits that scale with your plan. We set default meeting policies (who can join without approval, whether external guests can present) so admins aren't managing exceptions meeting by meeting.

Admin Console

Device & user management

The central control panel for provisioning users, enrolling devices, and applying org-wide policy. We structure organizational units to match departments, set enrollment rules for company and BYOD devices, and apply policy by group instead of one-size-fits-all.

CoversOrg unit structure, device enrollment rules, policy groups by department

  • Delegated admin roles by team

eDiscovery & retention

Retention policies, legal holds, and eDiscovery search across Gmail, Drive, and Chat data. Retention rules are set at the org unit level so different departments can follow different retention schedules where regulations require it.

  • eDiscovery search across services
  • Exportable audit logs for compliance review

Cloud Identity

SSO & multi-factor auth

Single sign-on and multi-factor authentication for Workspace and the third-party apps your team logs into every day. We roll out 2-Step Verification and security keys, connect SSO for your other business apps, and clean up inactive or over-privileged accounts before they become a liability.

Covers2-Step Verification rollout, SSO app connections, inactive account cleanup

  • Inactive account review and cleanup
