Our approach

Rua Technologies exists to remove the friction between what a business needs from its technology and what actually gets procured, deployed, and supported. Instead of juggling separate vendor reps, resellers, and support desks for productivity software, cloud infrastructure, and security tooling, our clients work with one team that understands the full stack and owns outcomes across it.

Where we fit in

We work as an extension of internal IT and finance teams — not a replacement for them — handling the procurement mechanics, technical configuration, and renewal governance so internal staff can focus on the business priorities that actually need their attention. Headquartered in Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh, we support organizations throughout Central India, Chhattisgarh, and the Delhi NCR with dedicated on-ground presence, and extend that same standard of coverage to businesses anywhere in India remotely.

FAQ

Common questions about working with us.

QAre you a genuine, authorized partner for the vendors you sell?

Yes — our partner and reseller status with Microsoft, Google, Adobe, AWS, Autodesk, Trend Micro, and Seqrite can be verified directly with each vendor. We don't sell through gray-market or unauthorized channels.

QDo you only work with businesses in Madhya Pradesh?

No. We have dedicated on-ground presence across Central India, Chhattisgarh, and the Delhi NCR, and support businesses anywhere else in India remotely with the same standard of service.

QWe already have an internal IT team — can you still help?

Yes. Most of our clients have an internal IT or finance team; we work alongside them to handle procurement, vendor escalation, and specialist deployment work rather than replacing them.

QHow is pricing structured?

Licensing is priced at the vendor's published rates through our partner channel; project and support work is quoted based on scope after a discovery call. There's no markup hidden in the license cost.

QWhat happens after a project goes live?

Every engagement includes a handover to our managed support team, who track renewal dates, monitor security posture, and stay available for ongoing questions — not just a one-time deployment.
