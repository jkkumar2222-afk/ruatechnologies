FAQ

Common questions about support.

QWhat's the difference between support and managed services?

Support covers day-to-day help desk requests and troubleshooting; managed services adds proactive 24×7 monitoring, patching, and infrastructure oversight on top.

QHow do we submit a support request?

Via email, WhatsApp, or our ticketing system, whichever is easiest for your team — all requests are tracked to resolution.

QDo you support platforms you didn't originally deploy?

In most cases, yes. We'll assess your environment during onboarding and let you know if there are any limitations.

QWhat's your typical response time?

Standard tickets are triaged the same business day; urgent issues are escalated immediately.

QCan you handle onboarding and offboarding for new and departing staff?

Yes. We handle account provisioning, device setup, access assignment, and secure offboarding as part of ongoing support.
