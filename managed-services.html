Coverage that goes beyond a help desk ticket queue.

01Monitor

24×7 monitoring & alerting

Continuous monitoring of servers, endpoints, and cloud services with alerts routed before issues become outages.

  • Server and endpoint health monitoring
  • Automated alerting and escalation
  • Uptime and performance tracking
  • Proactive issue remediation
  • Monthly health reports

Best for: Businesses that need problems caught before users notice them.

Typical timeline: Monitoring coverage is typically live within 1–2 weeks of onboarding.

02Support

Help desk & end-user support

Responsive ticketing and remote troubleshooting so your team stays productive instead of waiting on hold.

  • Ticketed help desk support
  • Remote troubleshooting
  • Onboarding and offboarding setup
  • Password and access resets
  • Escalation to specialist teams when needed

Best for: Organizations that want predictable, responsive day-to-day support for their staff.

Typical timeline: Support access begins from day one of onboarding.

03Maintain

Patch & update management

Scheduled patching and updates across servers, endpoints, and applications, tested before wide rollout.

  • OS and application patch management
  • Firmware and driver updates
  • Patch testing before deployment
  • Update compliance reporting
  • Emergency patching for critical vulnerabilities

Best for: Teams that need patching handled consistently, not reactively after an incident.

Typical timeline: Ongoing, scheduled monthly with emergency patches applied as needed.

04Coordinate

One point of contact for license renewals, vendor support tickets, and IT asset tracking across your stack.

  • Vendor and license renewal coordination

  • Monthly reporting and reviews

Best for: Businesses juggling multiple vendors who want a single point of accountability.

05Secure

Ongoing visibility into your security posture across endpoints, identities, and systems, so gaps get caught during a monthly review instead of during an incident.

  • Monthly security posture reporting

Regular business reviews that translate ticket volume and system health into a clear view of where your IT budget and roadmap should go next.

FAQ

Common questions about managed services.

QWhat does 'managed services' actually include?

24×7 monitoring, help desk support, patch management, backup oversight, vendor coordination, and monthly reporting — a full outsourced IT operations layer.

QDo we need to replace our current IT setup to use managed services?

No. We work with your existing infrastructure and licensing, and can complement or gradually take over responsibilities from an internal team if you have one.

QHow fast is support response?

Help desk tickets are triaged and responded to the same business day, with emergency issues escalated immediately.

QCan you manage a hybrid environment with cloud and on-prem systems?

Yes. Monitoring and support cover both cloud services and on-premises servers and network equipment.

QIs managed services billed per device or per user?

Pricing depends on environment size and scope — we'll size it based on device count, user count, and the services you need after a short discovery call.
