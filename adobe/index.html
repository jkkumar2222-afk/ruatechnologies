Creative Cloud & Acrobat plans

Creative and document tools, licensed the right way.

Business plans

Most licensed

Creative Cloud All Apps

Full app bundle

The complete Creative Cloud collection — Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere Pro, and every other desktop and mobile app. We handle license assignment and reassignment as staff join or leave, keep the Admin Console tidy, and help teams decide when All Apps genuinely pays off versus licensing single apps instead.

Included20+ desktop and mobile apps, 100GB cloud storage, Adobe Fonts, Adobe Portfolio
  • Includes Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere Pro, After Effects, InDesign, and 15+ more apps
  • Licensed per named user, with reassignment as staff join or leave
  • Business and enterprise editions available
  • Adobe Firefly generative AI credits included
  • Cloud documents and Libraries for cross-app asset sharing
  • Admin Console visibility into license usage and app activity
  • Best for teams using three or more Adobe apps regularly

Creative Cloud Single App

Individual app

Access to one Creative Cloud app — such as Photoshop or Premiere Pro — for teams that only need a single tool. We review actual usage across your team first, since a handful of All Apps licenses often costs less than many single-app licenses once the numbers are worked out.

IncludedOne desktop/mobile app of choice, 100GB cloud storage, Adobe Fonts
  • One desktop/mobile app of choice — Photoshop, Premiere Pro, Illustrator, and others
  • 100GB cloud storage and Adobe Fonts included
  • Adobe Firefly generative AI credits included
  • Lower cost than the full suite
  • Good fit for single-discipline roles
  • Can mix single-app licenses across a team
  • Usage review to confirm it's the cheaper option
  • Straightforward upgrade path to All Apps if usage grows

Adobe Acrobat Standard

Core PDF tools

Core PDF creation, editing, and conversion tools for teams that need reliable document handling. We license it where Acrobat Pro's advanced redaction and comparison tools aren't needed, keeping cost down for general office staff who mainly create, edit, and export PDFs day to day.

IncludedPDF creation and editing, form filling, basic export to Word/Excel
  • PDF creation, editing, and organizing tools
  • Form filling and basic e-sign requests
  • Export PDFs to Word, Excel, and PowerPoint
  • Windows only
  • Lower cost than Acrobat Pro
  • Good fit for general office use
  • Straightforward upgrade path to Pro if needs grow

Adobe Acrobat Pro

Advanced PDF & e-signature

Full PDF editing plus advanced features like redaction, document comparison, and built-in e-signatures. We set it up for teams handling contracts, legal documents, or anything requiring redaction of sensitive information before a file goes out the door, on both Windows and Mac.

IncludedAdvanced editing, redaction, document comparison, e-signatures, Windows and Mac
  • Windows and Mac support
  • Redaction tools for permanently removing sensitive content
  • Document comparison to track changes between versions
  • Includes e-signature sends
  • Advanced export and PDF-to-editable-format conversion
  • Batch processing for high-volume document workflows
  • Best for contract-heavy, legal, or compliance-driven teams

  • Acrobat Pro and Acrobat Sign bundled together
