Introduction

What Is Microsoft Defender for Business?

Modern endpoint protection built specifically for the needs and budgets of small and medium-sized organizations.

Microsoft Defender for Business is an enterprise-grade endpoint security solution designed for small and medium-sized organizations. It combines next-generation antivirus, endpoint detection and response (EDR), threat intelligence, vulnerability management and automated investigation capabilities in a single platform. Organizations can protect Windows, macOS, Android and iOS devices while managing security through the Microsoft Defender portal.

Cyber threats continue to evolve, making traditional antivirus software insufficient on its own. Defender for Business helps identify suspicious behaviour, detect ransomware attacks, isolate compromised devices and provide actionable recommendations to improve security posture. When integrated with Microsoft 365 Business Premium, Microsoft Intune and Microsoft Entra ID, it becomes part of a comprehensive Zero Trust security strategy.

Roll out Defender for Business in phases — onboard pilot devices first, validate security policies, then expand organization-wide.

Common questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Defender protect against ransomware?

Yes — it includes dedicated capabilities to detect, contain and respond to ransomware activity in real time.

Can it protect remote workers?

Yes, devices remain protected wherever they connect, with policies enforced from the cloud.

Is it included with Business Premium?

Yes, Microsoft Defender for Business is included with Microsoft 365 Business Premium.

Can it replace traditional antivirus?

It provides modern endpoint protection with detection and response capabilities well beyond traditional antivirus.
